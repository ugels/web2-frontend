CarbonClicker
CarbonClicker is a collaborative web-based game project focused on raising awareness about environmental issues, particularly carbon emissions and climate change. Serving as a back-end developer for the project, I contributed to creating a real-time multiplayer experience where players could join teams representing either oxygen or carbon dioxide. The game simulates the battle between these two elements, with players working together to reduce carbon emissions or increase oxygen levels.
